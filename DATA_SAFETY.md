## Data Safety Labels (App Store / Google Play)

### 1. Location
*   **Data Type:** Approximate Location
*   **Collection:** Always Collected / Shared with Friends
*   **Purpose:** App Functionality (Friend Map)
*   **Privacy:** "Blurred Mode" available (randomized 300m-500m offset).

### 2. Photos/Videos
*   **Data Type:** Photos and Videos
*   **Collection:** Collected (Processed ephemerally)
*   **Purpose:** App Functionality (Messaging)
*   **Retention:** Deleted immediately after viewing (Ephemeral by Design).

### 3. App Activity
*   **Data Type:** Performance Diagnostics, Crash Logs
*   **Collection:** Anonymized data
*   **Purpose:** Analytics and Improvements

### 4. Account Management
*   **Data Deletion:** "Users can request the deletion of their entire account and all associated data through the in-app Settings menu."

## Privacy Policy Summary
Zenith is **Ephemeral by Design**.
*   **Data Deletion**: Snaps are stored in temporary memory/encrypted cloud buffers and purged immediately upon read.
*   **Spatial Privacy**: Location is only used for the Friend Map with optional "Blurred Mode".
*   **On-Device AR**: Facial landmarks for lenses (like "Neon Head-Dodge") are processed locally. No raw biometric data is uploaded.
